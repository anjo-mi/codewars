<h2><a href=https://www.codewars.com/kata/552cd8624a414ec2b2000086/train/javascript target="_blank">Deutschstunde</a></h2><h3>7 kyu</h3><p>Everybody knows a little german, right? But remembering the correct articles is a tough job. Write yourself a little helper, that returns the noun with the matching article:</p><ul><li>each noun containing less than 2 vowels has the article "das"</li><li>each noun containing 2/3 vowels has the article "die"</li><li>each noun containing more than 3 vowels has the article "der"</li></ul><p>Caution: Vowels are "a,e,i,o,u". Umlaute (ä ö ü) are also being counted!</p><p>(This Kata is a joke, there is no such grammar rule!)</p>
