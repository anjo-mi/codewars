<h2><a href=https://www.codewars.com/kata/5ff2093d375dca00170057bc/train/typescript target="_blank">Dice Rotation</a></h2><h3>7 kyu</h3><p>You are given an array of 6-faced dice. Each die is represented by its face up.</p><p>Calculate the minimum number of rotations needed to make all faces the same.</p><p><code>1</code> will require one rotation to have <code>2</code>, <code>3</code>, <code>4</code> and <code>5</code> face up, but would require two rotations to make it the face <code>6</code>, as <code>6</code> is the opposite side of <code>1</code>.</p><p>The opposite side of <code>2</code> is <code>5</code> and <code>3</code> is <code>4</code>.</p><h2 id="examples">Examples</h2><pre><code>dice = {1, 1, 1, 1, 1, 6} --&gt; 2:rotate 6 twice to get 1dice = {1, 2, 3} --&gt; 2:2 rotations are needed to make all faces either 1, 2, or 3dice = {3, 3, 3} --&gt; 0:all faces are already identicaldice = {1, 6, 2, 3} --&gt; 3:rotate 1, 6 and 3 once to make them all 2</code></pre>
