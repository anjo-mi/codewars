<h2><a href=https://www.codewars.com/kata/5904be220881cb68be00007d/train/typescript target="_blank">Plenty of Fish in the Pond</a></h2><h3>6 kyu</h3><h2 id="introduction">Introduction</h2><pre style="white-space: pre-wrap;white-space: -moz-pre-wrap;white-space: -pre-wrap;white-space: -o-pre-wrap;word-wrap: break-word;">Fish are an integral part of any ecosystem. Unfortunately, fish are often seen as high maintenance. Contrary to popular belief, fish actually reduce pond maintenance as they graze on string algae and bottom feed from the pond floor. They also make very enjoyable pets, providing hours of natural entertainment.</pre><center><img alt="Driving" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/adrianeyre/codewars/master/Ruby/Authored/fish.jpg"></center><h2 id="task">Task</h2><p>In this Kata you are fish in a pond that needs to survive by eating other fish. You can only eat fish that are the same size or smaller than yourself. You must create a function that takes a shoal of fish as an input string. From this you must work out how many fish you can eat and ultimately the size you will grow to and return this as an integer.</p><h2 id="rules">Rules</h2><ol><li>Your size starts at <code>1</code></li><li>The shoal string will contain fish integers between <code>0-9</code></li><li><code>0</code> = algae and wont help you feed.</li><li>The fish integer represents the size of the fish <code>(1-9)</code>.</li><li>You can only eat fish the <strong>same</strong> size or <strong>less</strong> than yourself.</li><li>You can eat the fish in any order you choose to maximize your size.</li><li>You can and only eat each fish once.</li><li>The bigger fish you eat, the faster you grow. A size 2 fish equals two size 1 fish, size 3 fish equals three size 1 fish, and so on.</li><li>Your size increments by one each time you reach the amounts below.</li></ol><h2
